Course Description

This course introduces students to the fundamental concepts of Information Systems (IS) and their role in supporting business operations and decision-making in modern organizations. It covers the components, evolution, and applications of information systems, including hardware, software, networks, operating systems, enterprise systems, and office productivity tools. The course also emphasizes information management, data quality, security, ethics, and emerging technologies used in today's digital enterprises.
